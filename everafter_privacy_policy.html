EVERAFTER PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how EverAfter Dating (operated by FFB Investments) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services. By using EverAfter, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: name, email address, profile photos, and profile details you provide.
  • Usage Data: interactions, matches, messages, and preferences to improve recommendations.
  • Device & Technical Data: device type, operating system, IP address, and diagnostics for service operation.
  • Location Data: when enabled, approximate location used for discovering nearby matches.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the service, including matching and messaging features.
  • To personalize your experience and present relevant profiles and content.
  • To investigate and prevent fraud, abuse, or other prohibited behavior.
  • To communicate with you about account activity, updates, and support.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, messaging).
  • Legal authorities where required by law, or to protect the rights and safety of others.
  • Other users: the information you add to your profile (photos, bio) is visible to other users per your privacy settings.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide and improve our service, remember preferences, and analyze usage. You can control cookies via your browser settings, but disabling them may affect functionality.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information as long as necessary to provide the service and for legitimate business purposes. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • You may access, correct, or delete your account information by visiting your account settings.
  • To request data export or deletion, contact support. We may ask to verify your identity before processing requests.

Children's Privacy

EverAfter is intended for users 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe your child provided us with information, contact support to request removal.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post changes here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance.
