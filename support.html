Getting Started

Welcome to EverAfter — here's how to get up and running quickly:

  • Create your account and complete your profile with photos and interests.
  • Enable location and notifications for best matching and messaging experience.
  • Use the "Discover" tab to find matches; tap to view profiles and swipe to indicate interest.

Tip: Verify your photos in the profile section to increase trust and visibility.

Common Questions (FAQ)

Account & Profile

  • Q: How do I reset my password?
    A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in screen — we'll email reset instructions.
  • Q: How do I delete my account?
    A: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This removes your profile and data.
  • Q: How can I verify my profile photos?
    A: In your profile, follow the photo verification steps to complete the process.

Messaging & Matches

  • Q: I can't send messages — what's wrong?
    A: Check your network connection and that the recipient hasn't blocked you. If persistent, try restarting the app.
  • Q: How are matches determined?
    A: Matches use your preferences, location, and interest signals — improving profile completeness helps results.

Safety & Reporting

  • If you see abusive behavior, use the "Report" button on the user's profile — our team will review immediately.
  • Never share financial or highly sensitive personal information with other users.

Troubleshooting

  • App crashes or behaves oddly: Clear cache, update to the latest version, and restart your device.
  • Location not updating: Ensure location permissions are enabled for EverAfter in system settings.
  • Notifications are delayed: Check notification permissions and battery optimization settings.

Billing & Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are handled by the app store (Google Play / Apple App Store). For billing issues:

  • Check your purchase history in the app store account used for the purchase.
  • Contact support with a screenshot of the receipt so we can track and assist.
