Who we are

EverAfter Dating is an adults-only (18+) social and dating app focused on respectful connections, safety, and transparency.

Key features

  • Profile creation with photos and prompts.
  • Matching and messaging with user controls to block or report.
  • Safety-first onboarding with age confirmation and policy reminders.
  • Optional notifications for new matches and messages.

Safety and trust

  • Zero tolerance for harassment, CSAM, or exploitation.
  • In-app reporting for users, profiles, and messages; blocking stops all contact.
  • Moderation and enforcement to remove violators; repeat offenders are banned.
  • Advertising is supported; we honor ad ID reset/limit-ad-tracking settings.
